Who We Are




Gazu Group is a diversified business conglomerate with a deep strategic and research based business approach, an incorporation of excellent business practices and products with a vision to have a global audience footprint across all platforms.

The Group’s activities encompass:

  • The connection station – Free public wifi and mobile phone charge hub
  • Teanant cloud – Real Estate and Facilities Management Platform for Nigeria/West Africa
  • Trade ex – Physical Commodity trade facilitation//management application.
  • Trade Intel – Physical Commodity trading Intelligence/Research analysis and strategy consulting
  • Trade Finance – Financing of LC/SBLC, Loans for commodity trading
  • Geofencing Technology – Geofencing, Geolocation Marketing in various applications in Nigeria
  • Agro Comodity Trade – Sourcing of Organic Agricultural products for export: such as;

    ( CashewNut, Cocoa beans, Soya beans, Arabic gum)

  • Petroleum Trade – Sourcing of petroleum product for importation and distribution such as

    (D2,D6,AGO,JP54,JET A1,Condensate,Naphtha,)

  • Gemstone Trade – Sourcing of Gemstones from across Africa such as (Ruby,Diamonds,Aquamarine,Aquatic gems)

    - Also, precious metal (AU)Gold.

  • Military Products – Independent associates & distributors for defense/military products Nigeria
  • Business for sale – Business and Investment for sale brokerage
